About

Building the future of commerce

We are on a mission to make eCommerce accessible to everyone. Founded in 2024, Vendora empowers entrepreneurs worldwide.

Our Story

Vendora was born from a simple observation: starting an online store was too complicated. We believed that anyone with a great product should be able to sell online without wrestling with technology.

What started as a weekend project has grown into a platform powering thousands of stores worldwide. From solopreneurs to enterprise brands, Vendora scales with your ambition.

We are a distributed team of engineers, designers, and commerce enthusiasts united by one goal: building the best eCommerce platform on the planet.
