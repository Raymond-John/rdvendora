Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Vendora.

What is Vendora?

Vendora is a complete multi-vendor eCommerce platform that lets anyone create an online store, manage products, process orders, and grow their business.

How do I get started?

Sign up for a free account, choose your store name and theme, add your products, and start selling. The whole process takes under 10 minutes.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! All paid plans support custom domains. You can connect your existing domain or purchase a new one through our platform.

What payment methods can I accept?

We support Stripe, PayPal, Square, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and all major credit cards. More payment providers are added regularly.

Is there a transaction fee?

Vendora does not charge any transaction fees on top of your payment processor fees. You keep what you earn.

Can I have multiple vendors?

Yes! Our Scale and Empire plans support multi-vendor marketplaces. Each vendor gets their own dashboard and analytics.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your store will remain active until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.
